Welcome to Brickell Place

A vibrant, family-oriented community offering the perfect blend of waterfront luxury, exceptional amenities, and the convenience of Miami's premier Brickell location.

Our Community

Since 1975, Brickell Place has been home to families seeking an exceptional waterfront lifestyle in the heart of Miami's prestigious Brickell neighborhood. Our community comprises two elegant towers with 582 residences, offering stunning views of Biscayne Bay and the Miami skyline.

What sets Brickell Place apart is our strong family-oriented culture. We're proud to be home to numerous families and children, creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere that's rare in urban high-rise living. Our extensive amenities cater to residents of all ages, from toddlers to active adults.

Located directly on Brickell Avenue, our community offers unparalleled access to Miami's financial district, world-class dining, shopping, and entertainment, while providing a peaceful waterfront retreat with our private non-commercial marina.
